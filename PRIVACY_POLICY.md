# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Steam Hider is a browser extension that adds convenience controls to the Steam search page. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how it is used.

## Data Collection

Steam Hider does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal information.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or remote telemetry. It does not send extension data to any external server controlled by the developer.

## Local Extension Data

Steam Hider stores only extension settings in the browser's local extension storage. These settings may include options such as class names, cleanup behavior, timing values, and whether logging is enabled.

This data remains on your device and is used only to configure how the extension behaves on supported Steam pages.

## Website Access

Steam Hider runs on Steam search pages at:

- `https://store.steampowered.com/search*`

The extension reads and modifies the page content only to add hide controls, remove selected search result rows from the visible page, and trigger Steam's existing hide action when you choose to hide an item.

## Third-Party Services

Steam Hider is designed to work with the Steam website. Interactions with Steam pages are subject to Steam's own terms and privacy practices.

Steam Hider does not add any additional third-party services.

## Permissions

Steam Hider requests the `storage` permission to save extension settings locally in your browser.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension's behavior changes. Any updates will be published with the extension source or distribution materials.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the project maintainer through the repository where Steam Hider is published.
